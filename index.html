How It Works

Up and running in one visit.

We keep the setup simple so you can get back to running your business.

1

You reach out

Send us a message or give us a call. We'll ask a few quick questions about your office setup and get you on the right plan.

2

One-time setup visit

We come to your office, install our secure remote access software on each machine, and document your setup. Takes about an hour.

3

Remote support from day one

After setup, everything is handled remotely. Call, email, or text when something needs attention — we're on it.

4

Monthly check-in

We send a brief summary of what we did, what's running well, and anything worth addressing. No tech jargon.
